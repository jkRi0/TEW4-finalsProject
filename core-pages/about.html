About Discover Cabuyao

Connecting travelers with the authentic beauty of Cabuyao since 2020

Our Mission

To showcase the unique charm of Cabuyao while promoting sustainable tourism and supporting local communities. We strive to create meaningful connections between visitors and the rich cultural heritage of our city.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for authentic Cabuyao experiences, fostering cultural appreciation and economic growth while preserving the natural beauty and traditions that make our city special.

Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

Founded by local tourism enthusiasts with a passion for sharing Cabuyao's hidden treasures with the world.

2021

Community Growth

Established partnerships with local businesses and community leaders to create authentic tourism experiences.

2022

Digital Innovation

Launched our online platform to make Cabuyao's attractions more accessible to travelers worldwide.

2023

Sustainable Tourism

Implemented eco-friendly initiatives and sustainable tourism practices across our services.
