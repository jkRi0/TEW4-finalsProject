Booking & Reservations

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour through our website, by calling our office, or by visiting us in person. Online bookings are available 24/7, while phone and in-person bookings are available during office hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and cash payments. For online bookings, we only accept card payments and PayPal.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 48 hours before your scheduled tour. Please contact our customer service team to make any changes.

Policies & Guidelines

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 48 hours before the tour. Cancellations made within 48 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No refunds are provided for no-shows.

Are there age restrictions for tours?

Most of our tours are suitable for all ages. However, some adventure tours may have minimum age requirements. Please check the specific tour details for age restrictions.

What should I bring for the tour?

We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, water, and a camera. Specific items may be required for certain tours, which will be listed in the tour details.

Transportation

How do I get to Cabuyao?

Cabuyao is accessible by bus from Manila, with regular services from major bus terminals. You can also reach Cabuyao by private vehicle via the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX).

Is transportation included in the tours?

Yes, all our tours include transportation from our meeting point. We can also arrange pick-up from your hotel for an additional fee.

What local transportation options are available?

Tricycles and jeepneys are the main modes of public transportation within Cabuyao. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also available.

Safety & Health

What safety measures are in place?

All our tours are conducted by licensed guides, and we maintain strict safety protocols. We regularly inspect our equipment and vehicles to ensure they meet safety standards.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover any unexpected situations during your trip.

What health precautions should I take?

We recommend staying hydrated, using sunscreen, and bringing any necessary medications. Please inform us of any medical conditions before booking a tour.
